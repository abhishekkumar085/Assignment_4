What are HTML Entities?List out 5 commonly used HTML Entities..
Ans: HTML Entities are special sequence of characters used in html markup to represent characters that have a special meaning in html.
# It begins with & or end with ;
# Entities are frequently used to display reserved characters


5 commonly used Entities...

1.&amp;   => &
2.&copy;  => ©
3.&reg;   => ®
4.&trade; => ™
5.&lt;    => <
6.&gt;    => >
